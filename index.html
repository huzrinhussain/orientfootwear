Frequently Asked Questions

To order your favorite footwear from Orient Footwear, simply browse our collection on our Facebook or Instagram page. Send us a direct message with the style, size, and quantity of shoes you would like to order.

Our shoes are made from a variety of high-quality materials including genuine/ best leather, durable rubber, breathable fabric, and eco-friendly synthetic options. We prioritize both comfort and sustainability in our materials.

Currently, we only offer shipping within Sri Lanka. We are exploring options for international shipping and will update our customers when available.

To ensure the longevity of your shoes, we recommend cleaning them regularly with a soft cloth and mild soap. Keep leather shoes in a cool, dry place, and use shoe trees to maintain their shape. For fabric shoes, use a fabric cleaner to keep them fresh.

If the shoes don’t fit, you can request an exchange for a different size within 5 days of receiving your order. The shoes must be in their original condition and packaging.
